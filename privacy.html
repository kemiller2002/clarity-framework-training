Privacy • Data handling • Boundaries

Privacy Policy

This site is intentionally simple. The way your data is handled reflects that.

The Clarity Framework Training site is a static website. It does not use tracking pixels, behavioral analytics, or advertising cookies.

Any information you choose to provide is used only for the purpose it was given, and only to determine fit or facilitate communication.

Information you may provide

Only what is necessary, and only if you choose to engage.

If you submit an intake or contact form, you may be asked to provide:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your role or organization
  • High-level context about a decision you are facing

You should not submit confidential, regulated, or sensitive personal data through this site. If a conversation progresses to a private engagement, appropriate handling will be discussed directly.

How information is used

Purpose-limited and human-reviewed.

Information submitted through this site is used to:

  • Respond to inquiries
  • Evaluate fit for training or facilitation
  • Schedule conversations or sessions

Information is not sold, shared, or used for advertising. There is no automated decision-making or profiling.

Data storage and security

Minimal by design.

Because this is a static site, data handling is intentionally limited. Any information you submit is stored only as long as needed for communication or engagement-related purposes.

Reasonable measures are taken to protect information, but no internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your choices

Control stays with you.

You may request that your information be updated or deleted at any time by contacting the site owner directly.

If you do not wish to provide information, you may still browse the site freely.

Changes to this policy

Stability over surprise.

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in the site or how engagement is handled. Material changes will be reflected on this page.

Effective date: January 2026
