How it works

  • Request: short intake about the decision and stakes
  • Fit call: 15–30 minutes to confirm readiness
  • Schedule: cohort seat or private team session

We will say “no”

  • If you want validation for a decision already made
  • If you want templates instead of judgment
  • If the decision is primarily political or value-conflicted

A direct “no” is part of maintaining integrity.
